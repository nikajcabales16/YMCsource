THE VISION

BUILT NOT BOUGHT.

YMC.clo and Funded Auto Brokerage aren't separate entities. They are the two pillars of YoFunded.

This isn't a 9-to-5 story. This is a 3-year grind. Selling tees to buy tools. Brokerage deals to fund builds.

"The goal? A physical garage. A hub. A place where the Tri-State's sickest builds gather not for clout, but for culture. No 'takeover kids.' Just pure, unadulterated automotive passion."

This brand is about cars and a lifestyle. Streetwear meets car culture. For people who are extremely into cars. German engineering strictly highline, but all tuff builds are welcome as long as approved by me.
